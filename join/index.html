Prospective students and collaborators

I’m recruiting PhD students, postdocs, visiting students, interns, and research collaborators at the University of Miami. The group will work on secure AI agent systems, with a broad mandate: anything that helps understand how the web changes when software moves from humans clicking buttons to agents acting on their behalf.

I’m looking for curious, independent people with evidence they can make things happen: a paper, repo, system, demo, write-up, startup, open-source contribution, or project others relied on. If this direction feels close to what you want to help define, reach out.

Apply via Google Form → Or email guy@zyskind.com

Prospective PhD students should also apply through the University of Miami College of Engineering PhD program and name Guy Zyskind as a potential advisor.
